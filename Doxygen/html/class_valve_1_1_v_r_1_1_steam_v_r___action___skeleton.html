Valve.VR.SteamVR_Action_Skeleton Class Reference

Skeleton Actions are our best approximation of where your hands are while holding vr controllers and pressing buttons. We give you 31 bones to help you animate hand models. For more information check out this blog post: https://steamcommunity.com/games/250820/announcements/detail/1690421280625220068 More...

Detailed Description

Skeleton Actions are our best approximation of where your hands are while holding vr controllers and pressing buttons. We give you 31 bones to help you animate hand models. For more information check out this blog post: https://steamcommunity.com/games/250820/announcements/detail/1690421280625220068
