Valve.VR.SteamVR_Action_Pose Class Reference

Pose actions represent a position, rotation, and velocities inside the tracked space. SteamVR keeps a log of past poses so you can retrieve old poses with GetPoseAtTimeOffset or GetVelocitiesAtTimeOffset. Detailed Description

Pose actions represent a position, rotation, and velocities inside the tracked space. SteamVR keeps a log of past poses so you can retrieve old poses with GetPoseAtTimeOffset or GetVelocitiesAtTimeOffset. You can also pass in times in the future to these methods for SteamVR's best prediction of where the pose will be at that time.
